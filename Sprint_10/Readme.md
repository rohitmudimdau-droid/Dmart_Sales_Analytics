# EX10: Dashboard / Report Design

---
```
You are working as a Business Analyst in the Business Intelligence Team at DMart.
After completing data cleaning, lookups, PivotTables, charts, and KPI calculations, the management now wants a single interactive dashboard that provides a 360° view of business performance.
The dashboard should help management answer:

- Are we achieving revenue targets?
- Which regions are performing well?
- Which categories generate maximum profit?
- Which products are underperforming?
- What are the monthly sales trends?
- Where should business focus next?
```
---
